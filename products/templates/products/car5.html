Performance & Powertrain

The 2019 Beetle comes equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (TSI), offering:

  • 174 hp
  • 184 lb-ft of torque
  • 6-speed automatic transmission with Tiptronic and Sport mode
  • Front-wheel drive (FWD)
  • Fuel Economy: 26 mpg city / 33 mpg highway

Trims & Models

The 2019 VW Beetle is available in several trims to suit different needs:

  • S: The base model with solid features.
  • SE: The mid-tier model with upgraded comfort and tech features.
  • Final Edition SE: A special edition with unique touches.
  • Final Edition SEL: The top-tier model with premium materials and exclusive styling.

Design & Features

The 2019 Beetle blends retro styling with modern touches, including:

  • Rounded body shape with optional LED headlights and taillights
  • Available panoramic sunroof
  • Rearview camera
  • Final Edition models come in exclusive colors like Stonewashed Blue and Safari Uni

Interior features include:

  • Retro-modern dashboard design
  • Available two-tone leatherette seats
  • Heated front seats
  • Ambient interior lighting (higher trims)
  • 6.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Bluetooth compatibility

Pricing
