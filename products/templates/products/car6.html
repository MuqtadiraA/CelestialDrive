Ferrari F80 (Type F250)

Estimated Price: $2.5M – $3.0M

The Ferrari F80 (Type F250) is an unofficial concept hypercar, designed by Adriano Raeli and conceptualized in 2014. This cutting-edge design was aimed at surpassing the Ferrari LaFerrari, blending advanced hybrid powertrain technology with radical aerodynamics and luxurious, performance-focused features.

Performance & Powertrain

The Ferrari F80 uses a hybrid powertrain combining a 3.0L twin-turbo V6 engine with electric motors to deliver breathtaking performance:

  • Engine: 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 (900 PS / 662 kW / 888 hp)
  • Electric Motors: Three electric motors (300 PS / 221 kW / 296 hp)
  • Total Combined Output: 1,200 PS (883 kW / 1,184 hp)
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic (DCT)
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
  • 0-60 mph: ~2.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~310 mph (499 km/h)
  • Weight: ~800 kg (1,764 lbs)
  • Power-to-Weight Ratio: ~1.5 hp/kg

Design & Exterior

The F80 concept is designed for ultimate performance, with a sleek, aerodynamic exterior inspired by Formula 1 cars and stealth fighter jets:

  • Body Style: 2-door coupe with full carbon fiber body and monocoque chassis
  • Doors: Butterfly-style opening for futuristic access
  • Front End: Low-slung with aggressive air intakes
  • Rear End: Large diffuser, central exhaust, and minimalistic tail lights
  • Wheels: Lightweight alloy with aerodynamic covers
  • Canopy Roof: Jet-inspired glass cockpit design
  • Aerodynamics: Active front splitters and rear wing (conceptual)

Interior Design

Although speculative, the F80's interior is set to be focused on high-performance with advanced tech:

  • Seats: Carbon fiber racing bucket seats with 5-point harness
  • Dashboard: Fully digital driver display with customizable digital cockpit
  • Materials: Carbon fiber, Alcantara, anodized aluminum
  • Steering Wheel: F1-style with integrated driving mode selectors
  • Infotainment: Minimalist, performance-focused HUD and telemetry
  • Driver Interface: AR HUD (concept), providing real-time track data and assistance

Technology Features

The Ferrari F80 boasts cutting-edge technologies aimed at enhancing both performance and driver experience:

  • Hybrid System: Regenerative braking + kinetic recovery (F1-style KERS)
  • Aerodynamics: Automatically adjusting aero flaps and rear wing
  • Telemetry: Live performance data interface (concept)
  • AR HUD: Augmented reality overlay for track assistance
  • Lighting: Laser or OLED headlights and tail lamps (conceptual)

Pricing & Market

The Ferrari F80 is a concept hypercar, and while not produced, its estimated price would be within the following range:

  • Estimated Price: $2.5M – $3.0M USD (hypothetical)
  • Target Market: Ultra-wealthy collectors, hypercar enthusiasts, and private track users
  • Estimated Production: Limited to 50-100 units (hypothetical)
  • Competitors: Bugatti Chiron Super Sport, Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut, McLaren Speedtail, Rimac Nevera
