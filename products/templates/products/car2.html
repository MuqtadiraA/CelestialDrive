2025 Hyundai SANTA FE

Starting at $34,300

The all-new 2025 Hyundai SANTA FE blends rugged style with family-friendly practicality. With a bold design and spacious interior, this SUV is ready for both city streets and weekend adventures. Featuring hybrid and turbocharged engine options, the SANTA FE offers versatility for all lifestyles.

Performance & Powertrain

The Hyundai SANTA FE offers a choice of gasoline and hybrid engine options:

  • Gasoline: Up to 25 MPG city / 29 MPG highway
  • Hybrid: Up to 36 MPG combined (estimated)

Interior & Comfort

Spacious 3-row seating for up to 7 passengers, along with:

  • Class-leading rear cargo space with flat-folding seats
  • 12.3-inch digital cluster and 12.3-inch infotainment touchscreen
  • Eco-friendly interior materials, including recycled materials and optional vegan leather
  • Available panoramic sunroof

Safety & Driver Assistance

The SANTA FE comes equipped with Hyundai's SmartSense Suite, which includes:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist (FCA)
  • Blind-Spot View Monitor
  • Remote Smart Parking Assist (RSPA)
  • Highway Driving Assist 2 (HDA2)
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Safe Exit Assist (SEA)
  • 360-degree Surround View Monitor

Colors & Trims

Exterior colors include:

  • Ecotronic Gray, Terracotta Orange, Earthy Brass Matte, Abyss Black Pearl, and more

Trims available:

  • SE, SEL, XRT (off-road inspired), Limited, Calligraphy (luxury-focused)
