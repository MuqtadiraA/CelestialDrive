2023 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye Widebody

Starting at $70,000

The 2023 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye Widebody is the ultimate muscle car that blends raw power with iconic, retro-inspired design. It’s a modern legend in the making, with a wide stance, aggressive styling, and one of the most powerful V8 engines in a production car. Whether you're hitting the drag strip or cruising the streets, the Hellcat Redeye is built to deliver spine-tingling performance with every press of the pedal.

Performance & Powertrain

The Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye Widebody is engineered for those who crave power, speed, and performance:

  • Engine: 6.2L Supercharged HEMI V8
  • Horsepower:
    • Standard Hellcat: 717 hp
    • Redeye: 797 hp
    • Jailbreak: 807+ hp
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic
  • 0–60 mph: ~3.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~199 mph

Interior & Comfort

The interior of the Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye maintains the perfect blend of classic muscle car features and modern technology:

  • Leather performance seats with available heating and ventilation
  • 8.4" Uconnect touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Performance pages for real-time horsepower, G-force, and timers
  • Surprisingly spacious for a coupe with usable rear seats

Safety & Driver Assistance

While the Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye focuses on performance, it also offers essential safety features to keep you protected on the road:

  • Available Forward Collision Warning
  • Blind Spot Monitoring with Rear Cross-Path Detection
  • Rear backup camera with parking sensors
  • Adaptive cruise control (optional)

Colors & Trims

The Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye Widebody offers several bold color options and trims to suit your style:

  • Trims: Hellcat, Hellcat Redeye, Jailbreak, Widebody variants
  • Color Options (standouts include):

    • Octane Red

    • TorRed

Pricing & Market

The 2023 Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat Redeye Widebody is available at an affordable price for a supercharged muscle car:

  • Starting MSRP (2023): ~$70,000
