2022–2024 Land Rover Range Rover P530 Autobiography or SE

Starting at $107,000

The 2022–2024 Land Rover Range Rover P530 is the ultimate luxury SUV that combines refined elegance with extraordinary off-road capability. This flagship SUV from Land Rover maintains its reputation as a luxurious, high-performance vehicle suitable for both urban sophistication and rugged adventure. It features a sleek exterior with innovative design elements and a meticulously crafted interior offering unparalleled comfort.

Performance & Powertrain

The 2022–2024 Range Rover P530 offers an impressive range of engine options that cater to performance enthusiasts while providing impressive towing and off-road capability:

  • Engine Options:
    • 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 with mild hybrid (395 hp)
    • 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (523–606 hp)
    • Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV) with ~50 miles electric range
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD) with Terrain Response 2
  • Towing Capacity: Up to 8,200 lbs

Interior & Comfort

The Range Rover P530’s interior is designed to provide an unmatched level of luxury and comfort for both the driver and passengers:

  • Premium leather, wood, and metal finishes
  • Up to 7-passenger seating (with long-wheelbase option)
  • Executive rear seating with massage, heating, and recline options
  • 13.1" curved touchscreen with Pivi Pro infotainment system
  • Optional Meridian Signature Sound System

Safety & Driver Assistance

The 2022–2024 Range Rover P530 comes equipped with state-of-the-art safety and driver assistance features for enhanced peace of mind on every journey:

  • Standard adaptive cruise control, blind spot assist, and 3D surround camera
  • Available head-up display, lane keep assist, and driver condition monitor
  • Advanced parking assist and rear traffic monitor

Colors & Trims

The Range Rover P530 offers a range of striking color options and trims to suit your personal style:

  • Color Options:
    • Santorini Black
    • Fuji White
    • Eiger Grey
    • Sunset Gold
    • Belgravia Green
    • Custom SV Bespoke Palette
  • Trims: SE, Autobiography, SV, First Edition

Pricing & Market

The 2022–2024 Range Rover P530 is available in several trims, with the base model starting at a competitive price:

  • Starting MSRP (2025): ~$107,000

  • Market: Competing with other luxury SUVs like the BMW X7, Mercedes-Benz GLS, and Porsche Cayenne
